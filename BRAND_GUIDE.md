## Visual & Stylistic Guidelines

### Language Style

**Word Choice:**
- Use: custom, direct, local, affordable, quality, fast, flexible
- Use: we, you, your (personal pronouns)
- Avoid: revolutionary, game-changing, industry-leading (empty superlatives)
- Avoid: solution (overused in tech), leverage, utilize (use "use")

**Sentence Structure:**
- Short sentences for impact
- Longer sentences for explanation
- Fragments are OK when they add punch
- Lists and bullets for clarity

**Numbers & Data:**
- Spell out one through nine
- Use numerals for 10+
- Use specific numbers when possible: "17-day turnaround" not "fast shipping"
- Include units: 250mm, $25, 3-pack

### Content Formatting

**Headers:**
- Clear, descriptive
- Action-oriented when possible
- "Upload Your Design" not "Design Upload Portal"

**CTAs (Call-to-Action):**
- Direct and simple
- "Get Quote" > "Request Pricing Information"
- "Upload File" > "Submit Your Design File"
- "Contact Us" > "Reach Out to Our Team"

**Product Descriptions:**
- Lead with what it does, then how it's made
- Include material and finish
- List dimensions and specs
- Be honest about limitations

## Quick Reference: Voice Checklist

Before publishing any content, ask:

- [ ] Would I say this to a customer face-to-face?
- [ ] Is it clear what action the customer should take?
- [ ] Have I cut unnecessary words?
- [ ] Does it sound helpful, not salesy?
- [ ] Am I showing expertise without being condescending?
- [ ] Would a 3D printing beginner understand this?
- [ ] Does it reinforce our key differentiators (local, direct, affordable)?
- [ ] Is the tone friendly but professional?
